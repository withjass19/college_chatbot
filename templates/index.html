Principal's Message

Prof. Rama Arora
Principal

At PGGC 11, we embody the spirit of excellence and strive for continuous growth. Our motto, 'Higher and Still Higher,' reflects our commitment to pushing boundaries and reaching new heights. We believe that education is a journey, not a destination. We encourage our students to aim high, to dream big, and to work tirelessly towards achieving their goals. Our dedicated faculty and staff are devoted to providing a supportive and stimulating environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and critical thinking.

As we strive for 'Higher and Still Higher,' we remain grounded in our values of integrity, inclusivity, and social responsibility. We prepare our students to be leaders, innovators, and global citizens who make a positive impact in the world.

College Facilities

Swimming Pool

Swimming Pool & Multipurpose Gymnasium Hall, Cinder Track (400m), Hocky & Football Fields, Basket Ball, Volley Ball, Badminton, Netball and Handball Courts, Lawn Tennis Courts (clay and hard), Putting Pitch for Golf.

Library

The Library of college is well established modern libraries catering to the information needs of the academic community. The whole collection of the library is computerized and its OPAC is electronically accessible

Smart Classes

Most of the class rooms are Smart Class room. Campus is equipped with CCTV cameras, Electronic Display Boards, automatic electronic bell, Biometric and Face Reading Machine and Electronic Data Processing (EDP) cell
